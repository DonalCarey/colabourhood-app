Community rules

Keep local action safe and useful

Be respectful

Challenge ideas without attacking people. No abuse, threats, discrimination or harassment.

Protect privacy

Do not post private addresses, phone numbers, emails, documents, bills, or identifying details about other people.

Be careful with photos

Do not upload photos of children or private property unless you have permission. Avoid showing house numbers, car registrations or personal documents.

Use Colabourhood for neighbourhood action

Projects should help neighbours coordinate real-world improvements, shared resources, local campaigns or community activity.

No spam or impersonation

Do not create duplicate projects to manipulate support. Do not pretend to be another person or organisation.

Report problems

If something looks unsafe, abusive or too private, use the report button so an admin can review it.
