Welcome to Colabourhood

A map for neighbours who want to get things done

Colabourhood helps people in the same neighbourhood turn local frustrations and ideas into shared projects — from safer crossings and street clean-ups to playgrounds, tool libraries or group buying.

1
Find or propose a project

Use the map to see what neighbours are already discussing, or add a new project for a specific place or the whole neighbourhood.

2
Gather support and help

Neighbours can support the idea, offer time, skills, tools or funding pledges, and add useful updates.

3
Coordinate real-world action

Each project page becomes a shared record of the plan, photos, needs, supporters, pledges and next steps.

Neighbour-first safety: protect privacy, avoid personal documents or identifying photos, and report content that looks unsafe, abusive or too private.
